Many employers have matching gift programs to match charitable contributions made by their employees. Gifts from retirees may also qualify for a match. Participating in your company’s matching gift program is a great way for you to enable your employer to join with you in supporting your philanthropy to Pittsburgh Opera.
If you are a Pittsburgh Opera volunteer, your company may also have a Volunteer Grant Program.
Please contact Torey Gricks for more information about Pittsburgh Opera and increasing your support through leveraging your corporate match, or check out our Frequently Asked Questions section below.

Your company will then send its matching gift to Pittsburgh Opera.
