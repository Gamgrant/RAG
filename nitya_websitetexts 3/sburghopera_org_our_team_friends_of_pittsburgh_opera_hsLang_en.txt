FRIENDS of Pittsburgh Opera (FPO) is both a friend-raising and fund-raising body in support of Pittsburgh Opera. Membership in FRIENDS of Pittsburgh Opera is $75 per household of 2 or more, or $50 per individual. Your membership fee supports Pittsburgh Opera financially, and your participation helps promote interest in, and awareness of, Pittsburgh Opera in the community.
