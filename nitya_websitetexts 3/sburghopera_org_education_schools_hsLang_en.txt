IF YOU AND YOUR STUDENTS CAN’T ATTEND THE OPERA, LET US BRING THE OPERA TO YOU!
Opera Trunks are large, colorful steamer trunks on wheels (25" square by 44" high), filled with books, costumes, music, and lesson activities—all the materials needed to introduce opera. Trunk  package includes transportation, one month trunk residency, and a presentation by teaching artists at the residency location, all for the low fee of $500. If students can’t come to the opera, we take the opera to them!
Please note: New participants must attend an orientation before arranging booking dates.
